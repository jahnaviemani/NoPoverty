Amma Two Wheeler Scheme

This scheme provides a subsidy for working women to buy two-wheelers.

The government covers 50% of the cost or ₹25,000, whichever is lower.

It helps women in urban and rural areas to commute safely.

The scheme is open to women aged 18 to 40 who earn below ₹2.5 lakh per year.

It is especially beneficial for women working in fields like teaching, sales, and nursing.

Over 2 lakh women have benefited from this initiative so far.

The scheme aims to increase women's participation in the workforce.

Benefited People Targeted People
21% of the scheme's allocation is reserved for women belonging to Scheduled Castes,1% of the scheme’s allocation is reserved for women belonging to Scheduled Tribes,4% of the scheme’s allocation is reserved for differently abled women (across all categories).
