Bolsa Família Program

Bolsa Família is a conditional cash transfer program in Brazil.

The program provides financial aid to low-income families to reduce poverty.

Beneficiaries receive between R$89 and R$205 per month, depending on household size.

To qualify, families must ensure children attend school and receive vaccinations.

Over 14 million families benefit from this program annually.

Studies show Bolsa Família has significantly reduced extreme poverty in Brazil.

The program is credited with improving education and healthcare among poor communities.
