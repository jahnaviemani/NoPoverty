ELEVATE Unnati Program

The Karnataka government launched the ELEVATE Unnati program to support SC/ST entrepreneurs.

The scheme provides financial aid of up to ₹50 lakh for innovative startups.

It offers mentorship, incubation support, and market access opportunities.

Focuses on sectors like IT, healthcare, agriculture, and manufacturing.

More than 489 startups have received ₹116.73 crore in funding.

The initiative aims to boost economic independence among marginalized communities.

Government aims to increase participation of SC/ST entrepreneurs in mainstream business.
