CalWORKs - California

CalWORKs is a welfare-to-work program aimed at reducing poverty among low-income families.

It provides cash assistance, job training, and child care support.

Eligible families can receive up to $1,122 per month based on household size.

Parents are required to participate in employment programs to continue receiving benefits.

The program assists over 300,000 families annually across California.

Provides education and training to help parents gain stable employment.

Aims to break the cycle of poverty by promoting economic self-sufficiency.
